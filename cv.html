OBJECTIVE

The role of the IT developer is not only to do programming. Before coding, he must think about the constraints, the technical implications, the consistency of the functionalities requested... in short, a series of steps that requires a good spirit of analysis, logic and pragmatism. Team spirit and good interpersonnel skills : if the IT developer is autonomous in his work, he interacts regularly with colleagues to solve problems/malfunctions but also with customers, users of the software he develops. His ability to listen, his ability to adapt to his interlocutor and to make himself understood are highly appreciated.
